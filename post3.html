Today marked an exciting and educational day for the Class 9 and 10 students of Mahakali Modern Public School. They visited the army camp (Barrack) in Suklaphata, an event organized to provide real-life learning experiences.

The journey began early, with students gathering at the school, brimming with anticipation. The bus ride to Suklaphata was filled with chatter and scenic views. Upon arrival, they were greeted by army personnel who briefed them on the day's schedule and the importance of the camp.

Students toured the barracks, observed daily routines, and learned about the discipline and dedication required in army life. They watched a live drill, which showcased the soldiers' skills and teamwork. This was followed by a Q&A session where students asked insightful questions about military training and life in the army.

The visit ended with a group photo and a heartfelt thank you to the soldiers. The students left with a deeper appreciation for the army's role and the sacrifices made by soldiers. This experience not only educated them but also instilled a sense of respect and patriotism.
